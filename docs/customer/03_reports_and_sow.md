# Reports & Statements of Work

## Assessment Reports

### Internal Consultant Report
A comprehensive report for your team that includes:
- Executive summary with health score and revenue opportunity
- Revenue analysis by module with hours and dollar ranges
- Domain health score breakdown
- Detailed findings with remediation guidance and revenue impact
- Margin analysis based on your rate card

This report is marked **CONFIDENTIAL** and should not be shared with the end customer.

### Customer-Facing Report
A white-labeled assessment report for your client that includes:
- Health score with plain-language interpretation
- Risk overview with recommended action timelines
- Top priority recommendations
- Detailed findings grouped by module
- Next steps and engagement guidance

This report uses your organization's branding (logo, colors, legal text) and contains **no pricing information**.

## Statement of Work (SOW)

### Building a SOW

1. **Select Findings**: Choose which findings to include in the scope. Use quick filters:
   - "All Critical" — select all critical-severity findings
   - "All High" — select all high-severity findings
   - "Quick Wins Only" — high severity + low effort items

2. **Configure Engagement**:
   - **Time & Materials**: Hourly billing based on your rate card
   - **Fixed Fee**: Set price based on effort estimates plus margin
   - **Blended**: Combination approach

3. **Review & Generate**: Preview the SOW sections before generating:
   - Engagement overview
   - Scope of work (grouped by module)
   - Deliverables
   - Assumptions
   - Exclusions
   - Timeline with severity-based phases
   - Pricing
   - Terms and conditions
   - Signature block

### SOW Tracking

Track your SOW pipeline through the status stages:
- **Draft** — Generated but not yet sent
- **Sent** — Delivered to the customer
- **Under Review** — Customer is reviewing
- **Accepted** — Customer has signed
- **Declined** — Customer has declined

The SOW Pipeline view gives you a kanban-style overview of all SOWs across assessments.

## Branding Configuration

Customize your reports and SOWs with your organization's branding:
- Upload your logo
- Set primary and secondary colors
- Add legal footer text
- Preview branding on sample report

Navigate to Settings → Branding to configure.
