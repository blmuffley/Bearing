## How Bearing Works

Bearing connects to your ServiceNow instance, scans configuration metadata across all major CMDB domains, and produces a comprehensive health assessment in minutes.

![System Architecture](/01_system_architecture.svg)

### The Assessment Process

1. **Connect** — Provide read-only access to your ServiceNow instance
2. **Scan** — Bearing evaluates thousands of configuration items across eight dimensions
3. **Score** — Each dimension receives a 0-100 health score with weighted composite
4. **Report** — Receive detailed findings, maturity assessment, and remediation roadmap

Ready to see what Bearing can do for your CMDB? [Get Started](/getting-started).
