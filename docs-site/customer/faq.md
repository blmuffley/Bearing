## Need More Help?

Bearing includes a built-in AI assistant that can answer questions about your assessment results, explain findings, and guide you through remediation planning.

![Bearing AI Assistant](/screenshots/09_chatbot_open.png)

*The Bearing assistant is available from any page in the dashboard. Ask questions about your scores, get remediation guidance, or explore findings in natural language.*
